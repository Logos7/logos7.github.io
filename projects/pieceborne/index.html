Pieceborne

A 3D strategy-puzzle adventure in which a team of pieces explores corridors and chambers, activates mechanisms, avoids traps and searches for the exit.

About the game

Pieceborne uses the visual clarity of chess pieces, but it is not a digital version of classic chess. Each piece becomes a character with its own role, while the board becomes an adventure space.

The player leads a team through levels filled with doors, keys, chests, traps, enemies and environmental elements. Planning, cooperation between pieces and proper use of their abilities are essential.

Key features

  • a team of different pieces with distinct uses,
  • levels built from corridors, chambers and mechanisms,
  • environmental puzzles and tactical obstacles,
  • keys, doors, chests, traps and patrolling enemies,
  • clear, stylized 3D visuals.
Goal: a full commercial game released on Steam — without a public source-code repository.
